Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy

At Blii, your privacy matters. Were committed to protecting the personal data you share and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, and store your information.

Last Updated on July 7, 2025

What We Collect

When you use Blii, we may collect:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and device details

  • Saved content: Links, documents, images, videos, and messages you choose to share with Blii

  • AI interactions: Prompts and responses used within the app (to improve suggestions)

  • Usage data: How you interact with the app to enhance user experience

We do not access your personal files, contacts, or messages without permission.

Why We Collect It

We use your data to:

  • Save and organize your shared content

  • Provide personalized AI suggestions and tags

  • Sync your content across sessions

  • Improve features and performance

We do not sell your data to third parties.

How We Store It

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Only you have access to your saved content unless you choose to share it.

AI Privacy

AI suggestions are powered by models that process your input. We do not train AI models on your personal data without consent. You can manage AI preferences anytime in settings.

Your Control

You can:

  • View, edit, or delete your saved content

  • Disable auto-tagging or AI suggestions

  • Request your data or delete your account entirely

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact us at [email protected]
